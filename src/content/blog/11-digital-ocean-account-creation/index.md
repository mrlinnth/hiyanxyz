Just a simple guide to assist non-tech people.

## Steps to Create a DigitalOcean Account

1. **Register** at [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com/)

2. **Provide billing information** via credit card

3. **Establish a team account** (without converting existing account or enabling secure sign-in)

4. **Add team members** by entering their email addresses and sending invitations. Recipients must confirm via email.

5. **Assign appropriate roles** to team members. "Member" is typically sufficient for most use cases.

## Documentation

For more details, refer to DigitalOcean's official documentation on team creation and membership management.
