⚠️ Warning Signs & Red Flags

Watch out for these project dealbreakers that signal trouble ahead

Content Delays

Client cannot provide content timeline or repeatedly misses deadlines. This is the #1 project killer.

Scope Creep

Frequent "small" additions without timeline adjustments. "While we're at it, can we also..."

Budget Mismatch

Client expects enterprise results with small-site budget. Unrealistic cost expectations.

Decision Chaos

Unclear approval authority, design by committee, or decision-maker unavailable for meetings.

Skip Planning Phase

"Can we just start designing?" indicates lack of strategic thinking and will cause rework.

Communication Breakdown

Slow responses, missed meetings, or stakeholders bypassing project lead.
